Surgical intervention rehearsal system

Any surgical intervention involves certain kind of risks and might present a challenge even to the most qualified specialists.

Innovative method of preoperative intervention rehearsal allows considering patient’s individual characteristics (tissues, organs and blood vessels location), and preventing possible complications in order to reduce the medical errors rate.

Surgical intervention rehearsal systems are developed to improve effective decision making, shorten intervention time and simplify intervention performance planning.

DEVELOPED SOLUTION OFFERS:

  • Patient CT/MRI scans uploading
  • Automated data analysis and patient's anatomy 3D-model reconstruction
  • Necessary corrections (e.g. detection of small-sized objects such as tumors)
  • Planned intervention type designation
  • Automated individual clinical pattern and case (including possible complications) composition
  • Surgical intervention rehearsal performance
